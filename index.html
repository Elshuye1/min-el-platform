Privacy & Policy Framework

01. Data Integrity

Min-el employs end-to-end encryption for all business metrics. Your inventory, financial logs, and tithe records are processed via Firebase Firestore with strictly limited access protocols. We never view or share your private sales data.

02. Third-Party Protocols

Our platform integrates Google AdSense and Google Translate. These services may use cookies to optimize user experience. By using Min-el, you consent to our use of these essential third-party scripts to keep our services free and global.
